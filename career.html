Life At CACHATTO

CACHATTO INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED (CIPL) is a Bangalore based subsidiary of e-Jan Networks Co. Japan launched in 2017. In the year 2003, e-Jan Networks Co. established CACHATTO, a unified solution to provide secure access to enterprise data without compromising on user agility or corporate manageability.

Since its inception in 2017, CACHATTO India Pvt Ltd has come a long way. Our establishment in Bengaluru to market e-Jan’s innovative enterprise in India marked a significant development for us.

Our team in India has since worked diligently to innovate, create and promote CACHATTO in all of South-East Asia. The CACHATTO team is constantly moving forward with new creations while also keeping our existing customers satisfied through our product.
